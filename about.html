About Me

Dr. Thomas Faulkner is a strategic Human Resources leader and consultant with a track record of optimizing workforce development, organizational design, and process improvement. Currently serving as the Director of Human Resources for the City of Kenedy, Texas, he is also the Founder of Faulkner HR Solutions, where he partners with organizations to enhance HR strategy, leadership development, and operational efficiency.

With expertise in change management, process optimization, and data-driven HR strategies, Dr. Faulkner is dedicated to aligning people, processes, and performance for long-term success. He holds the Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) certification from HRCI and is a Lean Six Sigma Black Belt (LSSBB) (2022), reinforcing his commitment to efficiency, accountability, and continuous improvement in HR practices.

Dr. Faulkner earned his Doctorate in Business Administration (DBA) in 2025 from South College, where his research examines the relationship between professional development initiatives and employee motivation, retention, and adaptability in rural public sector organizations. Grounded in Human Capital Theory, his work explores how workforce development investments contribute to organizational sustainability in resource-constrained environments.

Beyond his professional and academic pursuits, Dr. Faulkner is an avid mythology and etymology enthusiast, continuously exploring the roots of language and storytelling. He also enjoys hiking and exploring U.S. National Parks with his wife and children. Committed to lifelong learning and collaboration, he actively engages in strategic discussions on HR innovation, leadership, and organizational excellence.

Mission

To create a collaborative space where municipal HR professionals and public sector leaders can share knowledge, tools, and strategies to drive innovation, efficiency, and growth. This platform exists to break down barriers to learning, ensuring that practical HR resources, training, and best practices are freely available to those committed to strengthening their organizations and serving their communities. By embracing open-access principles, this hub encourages unrestricted knowledge-sharing under a Creative Commons license, fostering a culture of collective growth and professional advancement.

Purpose

This hub is dedicated to empowering HR professionals through an open exchange of resources, templates, training materials, and certification guidance. By crowdsourcing expertise and making knowledge accessible, it fosters continuous improvement, problem-solving, and real-world application. Materials shared within this platform are available under a Creative Commons license, ensuring that HR professionals can use, adapt, and contribute freely, creating a cycle of shared learning that advances HR excellence in the public sector.

Philosophy Statement

Knowledge should be freely exchanged, refined, and applied, not locked behind barriers. True learning is a shared pursuit—a process where experiences, insights, and strategies build upon one another, strengthening the profession as a whole. HR is more than policy enforcement; it is a living discipline that thrives when professionals support, challenge, and elevate one another. By prioritizing accessibility, adaptability, and shared growth, and openly licensing resources, HR can drive meaningful change, creating workplaces where people and organizations evolve together.
