Cultivating a Culture of Continuous Learning: Examining the Multifarious Effects of Professional Development on Motivation, Retention, and Adaptability Amid Employees in Rural Public Sector Governments

Dissertation
This dissertation investigates how professional development initiatives influence employee motivation, retention, and adaptability in rural public sector organizations, using survey data from the City of Kenedy, Texas. Grounded in Human Capital Theory and analyzed through multiple regression models, the study offers empirical insights for designing targeted workforce strategies in resource-constrained municipal settings.
